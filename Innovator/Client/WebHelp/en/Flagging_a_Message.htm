Flagging a Message

A user can flag or unflag any message within the discussion panel..

To flag a message, click on the Flag link. The flag changes its color from grey to yellow to indicate the message is flagged by a user.

To unflag a message, click on the Unflag link.

Click on the number hyperlink to open a display of the people who have flagged the message. This is shown in the figure below:

Note:  A user can follow messages that are flagged by specific users. For more information on this, click on Follow.
