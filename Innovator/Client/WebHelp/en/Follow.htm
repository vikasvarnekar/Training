Follow

The Follow command in the Bookimarks pane enables you to follow other users.

To do this, add new bookmarks to the Messages From, Flagged By, and Forums bookmarks in the Bookmarks panel. You can follow:

  • Messages created by a specific user

  • Messages flagged by a specific user

  • Messages that belong to a specific forum

Adding New Bookmarks

  1. Click the People button in the Bookmarks panel.

            The Follow dialog box appears:

   2.  Start typing the name of the person you wish to follow and then click on the name when it comes up. It is added to the list of people you
        are following.
