Claiming or Unclaiming an Item

You can claim or unclaim an Item if you have the required access rights.  When you claim an Item, it prevents other users from making changes to that Item.  After you unclaim the Item, you or another user may claim the Item to submit additional changes to it.

Claiming an Item

There are multiple options to claim an Item.  From the main grid, select the Item to be claimed, and then select one of the following choices:

  • In an open item window click  . A green flag  appears next to the item name.
  • In the Search grid, right click on an item and select Claim from the context menu. A green flag appears next to the item in the Search grid.

Unclaiming an Item

There are multiple options to unlock an Item. From the main grid, select the Item to be unlocked, and then opt for one of the following choices:

You can only unlock Items that are locked by you. You cannot unlock an Item that is locked by other user.  Administrators may override the lock and unlock the Item.

  • In the grid, click the right mouse button (right-click) to open the context menu and click Unclaim.
  • In an open Item window, you can click  on the Item toolbar.
