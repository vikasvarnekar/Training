Forum Permissions

The ability to add or remove items from a Forum, or set the sharing for a Forum, is restricted to the Owner of the Forum and the administrator. Other users who have access to the Forum can see the messages in the Forum and post messages to it, but they cannot change the item content of the Forum or change the sharing settings on it.

Users cannot remove any of the “Default Group” of Bookmarks -- All Bookmarks, Messages from me and Flagged by me.

There is no end-user functionality to inactivate or delete a Forum.  The user can remove the Forum from his Bookmarks.
