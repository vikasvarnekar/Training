Forums

Forums  allow secure social discussion on any topic. They contain items and saved searches. Forums do not contain other Forums but they contain discussion on specific topics.

Clicking on Forums shows all aggregated messages from all contained items.   Clicking on one of the items or saved searches inside a Forum will just show the message content from that item.

  • Personal

  • Shared with specific users and groups

  • Available to everyone
