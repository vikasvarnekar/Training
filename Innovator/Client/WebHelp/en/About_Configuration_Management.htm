About Configuration Management

Configuration Management is the process of managing an organization's products, facilities and processes by managing their requirements, including changes, and assuring that results conform in each case.

All Items in Innovator have a LifeCycle and Items may be versionable.  Changes to versionable Items in Innovator are implemented using Versions, Revisions and behaviors which are all part of the Innovator Framework
