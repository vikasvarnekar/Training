Dashboards

Dashboards are graphics compiled from the data in the Aras Innovator database, to help management gain a good overall picture of how the business is progressing. In order to view these dashboards, the Adobe SVG Viewer must be installed. See prerequisites for instructions.

There are five dashboards that come standard with Aras Innovator:

  1. Design to Goal
  2. Engineering Efficiency
  3. Engineering Optimization
  4. Product Innovation
  5. Time to Manufacturing
