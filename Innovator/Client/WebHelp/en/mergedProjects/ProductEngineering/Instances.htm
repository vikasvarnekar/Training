Instances

The instance metadata is used primarily in electronic diagrams, and consists of the Reference Designator, the XYZ location, the angle, and the side (Top or Bottom) from which the location is measured.  For example, a circuit board may contain 4 equal resistors. The reference designator is a label, assigned to each instance of the resistor. The resistor is further identified by its location on the diagram.
