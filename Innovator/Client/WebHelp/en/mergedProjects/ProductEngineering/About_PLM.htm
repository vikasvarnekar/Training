About Product Engineering

Aras Product Engineering is a business ready solution for product definition and engineering change management across your company and with suppliers and customers.

Development metrics are collected as a result of ongoing engineering processes for the measurement of progress against goals and presented in graphs, reports, and scorecards for visual management.

Benefits

  • Visibility into key development indicators increases engineering effectiveness and provides a basis for continuous improvement

  • Manage product definition processes securely online to develop high quality products on time and on budget

  • Engineering change management reduces cycle times and streamlines business operations

  • Control product information release between departments, divisions, suppliers, and customers for effective coordination

  • Reuse of product and process information increases productivity and controls costs

  • Industry best practices for configuration management and engineering change are incorporated using the CMII principles and processes

  • Unparalleled flexibility enables application modification to address proprietary engineering processes and specific competitive practices
