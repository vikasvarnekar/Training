Product Engineering Reports

Product Engineering Reports are installed with the Product Engineering solution and allow the user to see complex multi-level information in a well organized layout. For any Part there are three types of reports available:

  • Multilevel BOM (Bill of Materials) Report - which displays all parts in the selected part's BOM along with their position in the hierarchy and quantity.

  • BOM Quantity Rollup Report - which displays all individual part quantities necessary from the top level part selected.

  • BOM Costing Report - which calculates the cost of the part, based on the actual or estimate costs of its children parts.

Please see the Aras Innovator Installation Guide for general Aras Innovator client and server configuration instructions.

Accessing These Reports

  1. Select the part for which you want a report.

  2. Right click and select the report you want from the popup menu.

Or

Select the report you want from the Reports menu on the main menu bar.
