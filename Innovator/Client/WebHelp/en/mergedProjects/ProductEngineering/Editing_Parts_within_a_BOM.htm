Editing Parts within a BOM

Once you have a part with a BOM table, it may look something like this:

The parent part shown is an F41 Turbine. It is an assembly, and contains the parts shown above in its BOM. The 4th part, 12Casing Assembly, is also an assembly and will most likely have a list of parts in its own BOM. For this example, we will assume that all the parts necessary for every parent part have been created separately. So, here we can use the nested editing to add the parts to their corresponding BOMs. We will use the turbine assembly as an example.

Editing a part from its parent's BOM tab

  1. Open the part to edit it. In our case, the F41 turbine was opened, as shown above.

  2. Under the BOM tab, select the child part (12 Casing Assembly), right click on it and select Open from the menu that appears. 

  3. From the popup menu select View "Parts".

  4. You will see a Parts form, for the child part that you selected, in our case for the 12Casing Assembly.  Notice that it is in View mode, not Edit mode. In order to add parts to the BOM, you must first claim it by clicking the Edit button .

In general, if you access a related part from its parent, it will be unclaimed. You then have to manually claim it, should you wish to edit it. If you are unable to claim an item, it may already be locked by another user, or you may not have update permission for the item.
