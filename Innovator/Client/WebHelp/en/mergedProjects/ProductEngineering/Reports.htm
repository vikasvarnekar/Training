Reports

Reports allow the user to see complex, multi-level information in a well organized layout. For any Part there are three types of reports available:

  • Multilevel BOM report - shows all the parts in the parent part's BOM, along with their position in the hierarchy and quantity;
  • BOM Quantity Rollup Report - displays all the parts necessary for the top level part and their quantities;
  • BOM Costing Report - calculates the cost of the part, based on the actual or estimate costs of its children parts.

  1. To access these reports, select Design>Parts from the Navigation pane. The following menu appears:

  1. Click Search Parts. The Parts grid appears.

  2. Click  to see a list of Parts.

  3. Right click on a Part in the grid and select the report you want from the popup menu.
