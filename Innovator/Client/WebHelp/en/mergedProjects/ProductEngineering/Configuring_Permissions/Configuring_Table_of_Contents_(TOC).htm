Configuring Table of Contents (TOC)

The folders and ItemTypes that appear in the Table of Contents (TOC) are determined by the identities associated with particular users. The TOC Access tab controls this visibility. As an Administrator, you can change the TOC access associated with an ItemType to meet your business needs.

The TOC Access accordion tab provides the specified identities with view-only access to this ItemType in the TOC. An Identity specified under Name can only view this ItemType in a TOC Category specified under Category.

The Can Add accordion tab of an ItemType defines Identities that can create items of this ItemType in the system, including creating from the TOC.
