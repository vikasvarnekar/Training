Products

The term “Product” and “Model” can have different relative meanings in different companies and industries.  In Aras Innovator, a product is defined as something that is bought by the customer. For example, for the Ford car company, one of the products could be the Mustang. Each product contains at least one model, which in turn has a one-to-one relationship with a top level assembly Part. In the Ford example, the Mustang model could be a Mustang GT. Sometimes a company will sell not only the actual product model, but also a tool kit, or a repair kit, or some sort of supplement. This can be defined as another model within the same product.

Here is how to define a product:

  1.  From the Navigation pane, select Design>Products. The following menu appears:

  1. Click Create New Product. A blank Product form appears:

  1. Enter the values for the following properties:

    • Product Number - this is the product identification accessible to the outside world.

    • Name - product name

    • Description - product description

  2. To add a model, click on the Add Row icon . A row appears in the Models grid. Enter the following information:

    • Model Number - the external model identification provided to the customers

    • Name - the model name, also available to the customers

    • Release Number - additional external identification for software products

    • Version Number - additional external identification for software products

    • Part Number - internal part number of an existing, predefined Part. Hit F2 to choose from a list of available parts.
  3. Click  to save and unclaim the Product.
