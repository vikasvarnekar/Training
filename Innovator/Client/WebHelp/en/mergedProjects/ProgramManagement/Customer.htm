The Customer Form allows all necessary contact information about a customer to be saved for easy reference. Here is an example of the form:

 

The properties of this form are:

  • Name - the name of the customer
  • Main Phone - the main phone number of the customer
  • Main Fax - the main fax number of the customer
  • Contact Name - the name of the focal contact
  • Address - the street and number of the customer
  • City - the city of the customer
  • State - the state of the customer
  • Country - the country of the customer
  • Zip Code - the zip code of the customer
  • Web Site - the web site of the customer
  • Description - the description of the customer
