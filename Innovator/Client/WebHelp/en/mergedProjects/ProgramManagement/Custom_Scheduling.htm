Custom Scheduling

Custom scheduling methods can be provided as a customization where standard forward or backward scheduling do not meet business needs. See Scheduling in Tailoring and Customizing

Milestone Scheduling has been discontinued in release 8.1; users preferred the explicit precedence provided by forward or backward scheduling rather than the 'implied' precedence' of milestone scheduling.
