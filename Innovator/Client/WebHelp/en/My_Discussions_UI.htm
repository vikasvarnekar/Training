Discussion Panel

The Discussion Panel for My Discussions within the My Innovator section of the TOC is as shown:

The Discussion Panel for My Discussions in the main window is almost identical to the Discussion Panel in an item tear-off window. Refer to Discussion Panel Toolbar and Components of a Message of the item windows to get more information on the properties of all parts in the discussion panel.

However, there are small differences in how some of their parts function. They are listed here:

  • Comment entry box and Comment button:  They are enabled only in Forums and items inside Forums or Bookmark groups.
  • Attach Link: This link will never appear within the Comment or Reply box, since the viewer is never displayed within the My Discussions main window.
  • Hyperlink on Referenced Item: The hyperlink on the Referenced Item of a message opens the tear-off window of the corresponding item, with the correct Revision and Generation as specified in the referenced item.
  • Hyperlink from thumbnail or snapshot: The hyperlink to show the snapshot image in the viewer will open up the tear-off window of the associated Board item and shows the selected snapshot in the viewer window.
