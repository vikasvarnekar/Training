Entering a Reply

The user can reply to a comment or to the existing messages within the discussion panel.

To reply to a comment, do the following:

  1. Click on the Reply link below the message. This opens a Reply Box.

  2. Type in the required text in the Reply Box.

  3. Click on Add Reply button to post the reply.

  4. Once the reply is posted, it is displayed underneath the corresponding message.

  5. Reply messages are shown indented and have an icon with an arrow.

  6. Click on Hide replies to hide the reply from the message list and Show replies to show the reply again.

     

Show replies and Hide replies

If the message contained more than three replies, for example five, clicking on Show replies link will initially show only three of five replies for that message.

The link now changes to Show all replies (7) and clicking on it will show all the replies for that message.

Once all the replies are shown, a Hide Replies link appears and clicking on it will hide all the replies for that message.

A Show replies link is displayed again below the message.

  

Note:   The replies are always sorted in the order “newest at bottom".

When you click the Reply link on a Reply message, you are actually replying to the original (top) message.
