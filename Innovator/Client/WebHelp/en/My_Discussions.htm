My Discussions

My Discussions is a screen within the My Innovator section of TOC. It shows all the social content the user is following and provides a Secure Social Interface within the My Innovator section of the TOC.

This p rovides a powerful, configurable mechanism to aggregate social content, including files, to related items. It includes discussion content and files from related Documents and CAD Documents.

You can access My Discussions from the TOC, by navigating to My Innovator  --> My Discussions.

Use the My Discussions panel to :

  • View messages for the selected item from Bookmarks

  • View messages for the selected item from Forums

  • Create a new Forum and send messages from the Forum

  • Search messages based on Bookmarks

  • Follow messages from a particular User or a Forum.
