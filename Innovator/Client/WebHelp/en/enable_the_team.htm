Steps to Enable the Team Item for an Item Type (Administrators Only)

These steps describe exposing the ‘team_id’ system property on a form for an ItemType.  They are generic and apply to any ItemType in the system.

View the form (edit mode) for the ItemType to which you wish to provide users the ability to use the team item.

Add the ‘team_id’ system property to the form.

Save the form.  Users may now associate a Team to instances of the ItemType.
