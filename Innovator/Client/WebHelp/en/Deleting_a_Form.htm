Deleting a Form

  1. From the Navigation pane select Administration>Forms>Search Forms. The search grid appears.
  2. Click  to see a list of forms.


  1. Right-click a Form row in the grid to see the context menu.  

  1. Click Delete All Versions. A dialog box similar to the following appears:

  1. Click OK.

Note: When an error message appears after the pop-up window it means that the item has not been deleted.  The name of the property corresponding to the ItemType appears in the error message as [itemtype_name property_name} and must be deleted from ItemTypes.
