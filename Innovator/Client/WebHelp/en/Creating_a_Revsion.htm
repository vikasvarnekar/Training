Creating a Revision

To create a new Revision:

  1. From the Navigation pane, select Administration>Revisions.The following menu appears:

  1. Click Create New Revision. A blank revision window appears:

  1. Fill out the following properties:
    • Name - the name of the revision.
    • Revisions - the series of new revision names to be used. These names should be sequential and easily recognized in terms of order and increment.
  1. Click   to save and unclaim this item.
