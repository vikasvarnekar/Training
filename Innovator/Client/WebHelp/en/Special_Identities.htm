Special Identities

There is number of special identities in Innovator which are reserved for specific purposes. The following table describes these identities.

Identity Name

Description

Administrators

Users who have access to system administrative aspects and items in Innovator. Usually the members of this identity have the most extensive access and permissions

All Customers

Customers who may need limited access to the Innovator application

All Employees

All company employees. Once the company structure is in place, the top departments Identities should be added as members to this Identity

All Suppliers

Suppliers who need limited access to the application

Aras PLM

Internal identity for Aras PLM

Authentication Admin

Grants rights to authenticated users as well as checking user account permissions to verify that these users have access to the appropriate resources

Authentication Administrators

Identity for calling authentication methods

Authenticators

A means of verifying a user's identity

Change Control Board (CCB)

Members of this identity decide whether or not proposed changes to a software project should be implemented.

Change Specialist I

Members of this Identity have control over various aspects of the workflow of PRs, ECRs, and ECNs in the Product Engineering Solution

Change Specialist II

Members of this Identity have control over some aspects of the workflow of the ECNs in the Product Engineering Solution

Change Specialist III

Members of this Identity have control over the auditing actions in the workflow of the ECNs in the Product Engineering Solution

Classification Administrators

CM

Configuration Management group, includes the Change Specialist I, II, and III identities

Component Engineering

Members of this identity have control over and access to Product Engineering items, such as Parts, Vendors, Manufacturers, Manufacturer Parts, etc.

Conversion Manager

CRB

Change Review Board - members of this Identity have control over different aspects of the workflow of the ECNs

Creator

The identity that created the item

Manager

Members of this identity have access to various Project Management items, such as the Project itself and its components - activities and WBS elements.

Owner

This identity is used by the Product Engineering items to specify an Assigned Creator, or the identity responsible for the technical content and review of the item

Process Planner Admin

QP Admin

Members of this identity have full access to various Quality Planning Items, such as DFMEAs, Process Planner, Quality Planning Library, etc.

Quality Planning

Members of this identity have specific limited access to various Quality Planning items, such as DFMEAs, Process Planner, Quality Planning Library, etc.

World

All users are automatically added to the World identity
